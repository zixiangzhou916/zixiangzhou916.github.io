I am currently a researcher at Xiaobing.ai. My research interests include multimodal human motion generation, diffusion models.

Before joining Xiaobing, I was a research and development engineer at Baidu IDG, and a senior engineer at Huawei ADS, respectively. I received my Ph.D. degree from Rutgers, the State University of New Jersey, where I was fortunate to be superivsed by Prof. Jie Gong in 2018. I worked as a research intern at Bentely System with Dr. Zheng Yi Wu 2017.

I'm seeking research interns on multimodal human motion generation. Feel free to send me an email if you are interested.
